THE DEVELOPMENT

Elevated livingabove Sea Point.

The Meridian is a collection of 42 luxury residences positioned on one of Sea Point's most coveted ridgelines, with uninterrupted Atlantic Ocean views from every unit. Designed in collaboration with acclaimed Cape Town architecture firm Studio Morales, the building prioritises natural light, ocean breezes, and the seamless connection between interior and exterior living.

Every residence is finished to VALT's signature standard — engineered oak flooring, bespoke Italian kitchen cabinetry, stone countertops, and floor-to-ceiling triple-glazed windows that frame the ocean like a living artwork. Underground parking and 24-hour concierge are included across all unit types.
